4. How to Check-in

(1) Submit a copy of your passport one day before check-in

Under Japanese inn regulations, we are required to record passport information for foreign travelers.
Please send a photo of your passport one day before check-in.
Personal information provided by guests will be handled appropriately and will not be shared with third parties without consent, except as required by law.

You may send the passport photo via email to yanyanli302@gmail.com.
Email subject: Great View House 3/21
3/21 is a sample check-in date (month/day).

At least one person in the group must be 18 or older. If all guests are under 18, a parental consent form is required.
If no one in the group is 18 or older, please submit a parental consent form. Please note that we may decline reservations for minors only.

(2) Unlock the door with a passcode

The passcode will be provided on the day of check-in.

How to Unlock:
  • 1. Enter the passcode on the keypad.
  • 2. Press the "unlock" button at the bottom right.
  • 3. Wait approximately 5 seconds until the unlocking sound stops before touching the door. Then open the door to enter.


Please Note:
(1). Carefully enter the passcode after confirming it.
Entering the wrong passcode three times will disable the keypad, preventing the door from opening.
(2). Always wait for the unlocking or locking sound to stop before pulling the door.

How to Lock:

Press the "lock" button at the bottom left of the keypad.
Wait until the locking sound stops before touching the door.
Ensure the door is closed.
