Main Features

A standalone house in Tokyo, exclusively rented to one group.

★ Rooms: 5LDK (5 rooms, living room, kitchen)
★ Amenities: 2 toilets, 1 bathroom, approximately 150㎡
★ Location: 3-minute walk to the nearest station, close to airports and attractions like Asakusa and SkyTree
★ Convenience: Fully equipped kitchen and washing machine, ideal for long stays
★ Suitability: Perfect for families or groups, accommodating up to 10 people
★ View: Enjoy river views and SkyTree's night view from the sunroom
★ Garden: Relax with a view of the green garden from the sofa

Convenient Transportation

1. The house is a 5-minute walk from Keisei Tateishi Station.
